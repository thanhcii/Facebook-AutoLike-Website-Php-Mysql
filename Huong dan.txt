Installation-
1. Create Database (User with all permissions)
2. Replace Database Details in (config.php, englishcommenter.php, hindicommenter.php, liker.php, punjabicommenter.php, recentusers.php, spamcommenter.php, z_like.php and token_check.php)
3. Open liker.php and replace (yoursite.com) to your domain.
4. Open verify.php and replace (yoursite.com) to your domain.
5. Open PhpMyAdmin and Import cookie.sql to your database.
Enjoy. :)

Advanced-
Open z_like.php and set your amount of likes. Default - 156
